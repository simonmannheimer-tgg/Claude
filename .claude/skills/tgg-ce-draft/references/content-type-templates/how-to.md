# How-To — TGG Augmentation Notes

Augments the base how-to template from `tgg-template-generator`.

---

## TGG-specific requirements

### Required upfront block (before Step 1)
Include a short block covering:
- What the reader will achieve
- Estimated time to complete
- Tools, materials, or prerequisites needed
- When to call a professional instead (safety-critical tasks: always include this)

### Australian safety and standards context
- If the task involves electrical work: "This should be carried out by a licensed electrician. Unlicensed electrical work is illegal in Australia."
- If the task involves gas: "Gas appliance work requires a licensed gasfitter in Australia."
- If the task involves plumbing: "Plumbing connections must be made by a licensed plumber in most Australian states."
- Include these statements naturally — not as a disclaimer block.

### Step format
Each step heading: `## Step [N]: [Verb phrase]` — e.g., `## Step 1: Remove the filter cover`
Each step body: action first, then explanation. 60–120 words per step.

### Completion signal
End with a brief "What to do if it doesn't work" paragraph before the FAQ. This is Australian retail-appropriate — customers want to know their next option.
