# EAV Explainer — TGG Augmentation Notes

Augments the base eav-explainer template from `tgg-template-generator`.

---

## TGG-specific requirements

### EAV mapping table
The centrepiece of this content type. Must appear by the second H2. Format:

| Attribute | What it is | Values to know | What to look for |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Name] | [Plain-English definition in 1 sentence] | [Range: e.g. 5–12 kg, 1–10 stars] | [Target value for most buyers] |

Minimum 5 attributes. Maximum 10. Cover only attributes that appear in product filters on thegoodguys.com.au or that buyers mention in search queries (use `seo-data.md` related queries as the source).

### Australian attribute context
- Energy star ratings: explain the Australian system specifically (1–10 stars, how it differs from EU labels)
- Water efficiency: WELS star ratings (if applicable — dishwashers, washing machines, shower heads)
- Dimensions: use centimetres (cm) and kilograms (kg) — not inches or pounds
- Voltage: 230V/50Hz context where relevant
- Standards: reference AS/NZS numbers where they define minimum performance (e.g., noise levels, energy use)

### Buyer decision summary
Place after the EAV table and before the FAQ. Answer: "Which attributes matter most if you…"
- Have a small household / large household
- Want the lowest running costs
- Have a specific installation constraint (size, connection type)
- Are replacing an existing appliance (compatibility)

Keep each answer to 2–3 sentences. Name the attribute, not the product tier.
