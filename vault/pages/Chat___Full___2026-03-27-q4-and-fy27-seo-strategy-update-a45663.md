- Mar-Sep 2025: Position 1 CTR climbed from 38% to a **64% peak** (post-migration recovery, less SERP clutter in the off-season)
- Oct-Dec 2025: CTR collapsed to 22-25% during BFCM. This is completely expected. Paid ads, shopping carousels, and promotional modules dominate Black Friday SERPs industry-wide. Position 1 CTR drops every retailer during peak.
