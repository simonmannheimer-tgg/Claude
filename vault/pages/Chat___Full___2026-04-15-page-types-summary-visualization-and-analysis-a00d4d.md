Three things worth flagging from this:

**JB Hi-Fi's structural advantage is unambiguous.** They use per-category EOFY sub-pages (`/collections/eofy-sale/tvs`, `/eofy-sale/fridges` etc.) stacked on top of the main hub. That means one URL competes for "eofy sale" broadly, and separate URLs compete for "eofy tv sale", "eofy fridge sale" etc. TGG's single `/eofy-sale` hub has to win all of those simultaneously.
