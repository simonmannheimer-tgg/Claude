# Research Prompt

Use this prompt to structure a research task before executing it.

---

**Research question:** [What specifically needs to be answered?]

**Scope:** [What is in and out of scope? What time period, domain, or depth?]

**Sources:** [What sources should be consulted? Files, web, documents, APIs.]

**Synthesis:** [How should the findings be combined and interpreted?]

**Output format:** [How should the findings be presented? Table, summary, list, report.]
