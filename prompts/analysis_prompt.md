# Analysis Prompt

Use this prompt to analyse a document, dataset, or situation.

---

**Context:** [What is being analysed and why?]

**Structure:** [How is the input organised? What are its main sections or components?]

**Key findings:** [What are the most important things you found?]
-
-
-

**Gaps:** [What is missing, unclear, or contradictory?]

**Recommendations:** [What actions or changes follow from the findings?]
